Featured Locations:

  • The Tower of London: A historic castle that has served as a royal palace, prison, and treasury. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site and houses the Crown Jewels.
  • Buckingham Palace: The official residence of the British monarch, known for the Changing of the Guard ceremony.
  • Westminster Abbey: A Gothic church that has been the site of coronations, royal weddings, and burials for centuries.

Each location offers unique experiences that capture the essence of London culture and natural beauty.
